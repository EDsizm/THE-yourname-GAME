THE yourname GAME

A game where you must follow the tasks your narrator sets upon you!, You must behave and be a nice person so you can get the best possible outcome, or you can be naughty and see what happens. And, whatever you do, DO NOT TRUST THE APPLE!

This is a game created by Team EdNan, composed by Stephano Naressi and Jeong Ho Lee. This is a year 1 Creative Project for our Creative Computing course.
